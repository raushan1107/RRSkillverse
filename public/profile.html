Our Journey

From one student to
a global community.

Our story began in 2014 as RR Education — with just one student and a vision to transform learning. From that humble start, we grew steadily, launching projects to boost not only skills but confidence and quality of life.

Over the years, our RR family expanded — students, alumni, trainers, and industry experts came together as one community. In 2025, we proudly rebranded as RR Skillverse — a reflection of our evolved mission and broader reach.
